🔒 Our Privacy Promise

We collect only what we need, we never sell your data, and we give you full control over your information. AirDrop Zone is built on trust.

01
Information We Collect

When you use AirDrop Zone, we collect the following information to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and authentication data when you register
  • Wallet Address: If you connect a Solana wallet (Phantom), we store your public wallet address only — never your private key
  • Activity Data: Mining sessions, tasks completed, referrals made, and coin balances
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type for security and analytics purposes
  • Google OAuth Data: If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google
02
How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively for:

  • Providing and improving the AirDrop Zone platform and services
  • Processing your mining sessions and coin balances accurately
  • Managing the referral system and distributing referral bonuses
  • Sending important service notifications (we do not send marketing spam)
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Preparing for the token generation event on Solana blockchain
03
Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase, a trusted enterprise-grade database provider with SOC 2 Type II certification. We implement:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row Level Security (RLS) policies on all database tables
  • Secure authentication via Supabase Auth with bcrypt password hashing
  • No storage of private keys, seed phrases, or wallet passwords
04
Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We share data only with:

  • Supabase: Our database infrastructure provider
  • Google: Only when you use Google Sign-In (governed by Google's privacy policy)
  • Vercel: Our hosting provider (no personal data stored)
  • Law Enforcement: Only if legally required by valid court order
05
Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your transaction history and coin balance data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us through our official Telegram channel.

🍪 Simple Cookie Policy

We use minimal cookies — only what is necessary for the app to function. We do not track you for advertising purposes.

01
Cookies We Use
  • Authentication Cookie: Keeps you logged in securely. Set by Supabase Auth. Expires after 1 week of inactivity.
  • Session Cookie: Stores your current session token. Required for the app to function. Deleted when you log out.
  • Preference Storage: We use localStorage to remember minor UI preferences like last visited page. Not a cookie but similar purpose.
02
What We Do NOT Use
  • No advertising trackers or pixels
  • No third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel)
  • No cross-site tracking cookies
  • No fingerprinting or behavioral tracking

Our cookie usage is strictly functional — nothing more.

03
Managing Cookies

You can clear cookies and localStorage at any time through your browser settings. Note that clearing authentication cookies will log you out of the app. Your account data will remain safe in our database.
